About

Dunedin Wheels is Dunedin's best car rental service. We provide high quality vehicles for rent at low cost for visitors and tourists of this beautiful city.

Dunedin Wheels started off in 1987, then known as 'Dunedin Vehicle Rentals'. Since then, our priority has been to provide the best service possible at reasonable affordable prices.
